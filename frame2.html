MUTHOOT INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY AND SCIENCE

Muthoot Institute of Science and Technology is a co-educational private institute that aims to impart quality education to the students. This institute was established in 2013 which is situated in Ernakulam, Kochi, Kerala. It is approved by the National Board of Accreditation (NBA) and is affiliated to APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University. MITS is the premier engineering college in Kochi that offers undergraduate and postgraduate engineering programs. The institute offer B.Tech and M.Tech course in various specializations. The minimum eligibility to apply for MCA programs offered at MITS Kochi is Bachelors degeree with any recognized university with 50% CGPA and Admissions are granted on the basis of LBS entrance exams
